Tap here for a reminder of how to save this app to your home screen

iPhone or iPad using Safari

  1. Tap the Share icon at the bottom of Safari
  2. Choose Add to Home Screen
  3. Tap Add

iPhone or iPad using Chrome

  1. Tap the Share icon in the top right
  2. Scroll and choose Add to Home Screen
  3. Tap Add

If you do not see this option, open the app in Safari and use the same steps

Android using Chrome

  1. Tap the three dot menu in the top right
  2. Choose Install app or Add to Home Screen
  3. Confirm Install

You may also see an Install button in the address bar

Android using Samsung Internet

  1. Tap the menu button
  2. Select Add page to then Home screen
  3. Confirm

Other browsers

Look in the browser menu for Install or Add to Home Screen

Installing adds an icon to your home screen and makes it function like an app

Printable Card Decks ▾

Over 340 cards are included in this printable bundle. These cards are pulled straight from the Playful Heart apps.

Each deck includes just a handful of favorites (most have 53 cards) so you're not printing hundreds of pages! The full prompt collections live inside the apps and continue to grow with new ideas over time.

Reminder: Little Moments for Big Laughs is a parent-only collection of simple, silly connection ideas. The other decks can be printed for kids to choose from and start the fun on their own!

Tip: To save paper and ink, print two-to-a-page or just the specific decks you'll reach for most often.

Ideas for using your cards ▾
  • Laminate, hole punch, and pop them on a key ring for grab and go fun
  • Fold them into a big jar and let kids pull a prompt whenever they want
  • Keep a mini stack in the glove compartment for easy car play
  • Store decks in a kitchen drawer for meal times
  • Clip a few to your bag for out and about fun, perfect for waiting
  • Keep a stack on your nightstand for bedtime giggles
  • Pick one card each morning as a Daily Play Challenge
  • Use them as a screen free reset when energy gets wiggly
  • Let kids shuffle a deck and deal out a card to start the fun
